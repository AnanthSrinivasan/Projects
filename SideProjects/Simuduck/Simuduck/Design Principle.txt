Design principles pertaining to this particular pattern or this application

1.	Identify the aspects of your application that vary and separate them
	from what stays the same.
2.	Program to an interface and not an implementation.
3.	Favor Composition over Inheritance.

Inspite of Inheritance being a powerful tool in OO design which gives reuse as its 
gift to developers. There are always better ways to acheive reuse.

			and make them interchangeable.
